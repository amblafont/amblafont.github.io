Ambroise Lafont

Myself

I am currently a postdoc in the Cogent team in Sydney. Before, I was a PhD student the Inria team Gallinette at LS2N in Nantes, supervised by Nicolas Tabareau and Tom Hirschowitz. Before, I was a computer science student at Ecole Polytechnique and graduated for the MPRI (Parisian Master in Computer Science).

My research interests mainly lie in Type Theory and Category Theory.
